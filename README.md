## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer retention and churn using the Telco Customer Churn dataset. The objective is to identify customer segments with the highest risk of churn, understand the key drivers of customer loss, and provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.

---

## Business Problem

Customer churn is a major challenge for subscription-based businesses because it directly impacts revenue and customer lifetime value. This project investigates customer behavior to identify why customers leave and recommends strategies to improve retention.

## Key Insights

- Approximately **26.54%** of customers churned while **73.46%** remained retained.
- Customers on **month-to-month contracts** experienced the highest churn.
- **Fiber optic** customers showed higher churn than DSL customers.
- Customers using **electronic check** payment methods were more likely to churn.
- **Senior citizens** had higher churn rates than non-senior customers.
- **Gender** had minimal influence on customer churn.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Promote long-term contracts through discounts and loyalty rewards.
- Improve customer onboarding during the first months of service.
- Review fiber optic pricing and service quality.
- Encourage automatic payment methods with customer incentives.
- Launch targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customer groups.
- Monitor customer satisfaction continuously.
